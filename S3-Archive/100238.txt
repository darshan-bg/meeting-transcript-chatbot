# Product Name

The product planned to be launched is called **LEGO World Creator**.

This is mentioned at the very beginning of the transcript:

> "We've spent nearly eight months exploring what we're currently calling *LEGO World Creator*."

The product is described as a **modular city-building ecosystem** where customers gradually build an entire living world by purchasing compatible expansion sets (schools, airports, transit systems, museums, neighborhoods, etc.) that can be added over time rather than being standalone, one-time purchases.
